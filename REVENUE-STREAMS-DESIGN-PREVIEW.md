1. **Cards slide in from left** (staggered: 0s, 0.2s, 0.4s)

1. **Card lifts up** (transform: translateY(-8px))
2. **Shadow increases** (deeper 3D effect)
3. **Border glow brightens** (cyan accent)
4. **Scale slightly** (1.02x larger)
