### What Changed
Added CSS filters to make the Isla Aguada Transformation video dramatically more vivid and stunning.

### CSS Filters Applied (UPGRADED)
```css
filter: contrast(1.2) saturate(1.5) brightness(1.08);
```

### Filter Breakdown
1. **Contrast: 1.2** (20% increase - UP from 15%)
   - Makes darks darker and lights lighter
   - Adds dramatic depth and definition
   - Enhances visual impact significantly

2. **Saturation: 1.5** (50% increase - UP from 30%)
   - Makes colors MUCH more vibrant and rich
   - Ocean blues become stunning tropical teal
   - Beach colors really pop
   - Golden tones are magnificent
   - **This is the sweet spot for luxury resort feel**

3. **Brightness: 1.08** (8% increase - UP from 5%)
   - Compensates for increased contrast
   - Maintains vibrant, inviting brightness
   - Keeps video luminous and appealing
