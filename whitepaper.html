08 Dual Utility Business Model

Most real estate tokens rely solely on asset appreciation. MayaRoyale introduces a Dual Utility Model that acts as both an investment vehicle and a functional currency within the resort.

1. Investment Vehicle

As the underlying 15-acre trophy beachfront property appreciates, the asset backing of each token increases. We project a conservative 15% annual land appreciation, driving long-term value for holders.

2. Hotel Currency (The Ecosystem Loop)

The MAYA Token is integrated into the resort's payment infrastructure. This creates a powerful economic feedback loop:

  1. Purchase: Guests purchase MAYA tokens to pay for stays, dining, and gaming to access exclusive discounts and VIP perks.
  2. Spending: Tokens are spent at the resort, generating revenue.
  3. Recycling: The resort recycles these tokens back into the market or burns them, managing supply.
  4. Demand: This constant transactional volume creates perpetual buying pressure independent of speculative trading.

15 Legal & Regulatory Framework

The Fideicomiso Structure

To ensure full compliance with Mexican law regarding foreign land ownership in the restricted zone (coastline), MayaRoyale utilizes a Bank Trust (Fideicomiso). The land title is held by a major Mexican bank for the benefit of the project entity.

Token Legal Structure

The MAYA Token is structured to comply with international securities regulations. It represents a contractual right to revenue and governance within the project entity, backed by the physical asset held in the Fideicomiso. We enforce strict KYC/AML procedures for all Series A investors.
