About Leading Hotels of the World

Leading Hotels of the World (LHW) is the most prestigious collection of luxury independent hotels, representing over 400 extraordinary properties in more than 80 countries. For nearly a century, LHW has been the gold standard in hospitality, curating exceptional experiences that blend authentic local culture with world-class service. Membership in LHW is highly selective, requiring rigorous third-party inspections with a minimum score of 85% and approval by their Executive Committee.

For MayaRoyale, this partnership represents a transformative validation of our vision and operational standards. LHW membership provides immediate global credibility, access to their affluent international clientele, sophisticated distribution channels, and brand association with iconic properties worldwide. This strategic alliance positions MayaRoyale within an elite network that drives premium pricing power, accelerates market recognition, and establishes our resort as a world-class destination from day one.
